Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for international trips and 4-6 weeks for domestic travel. This ensures better availability and pricing for flights and accommodations.

What is included in your travel packages?

Our packages typically include accommodations, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by package and are clearly listed in each package description.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we highly recommend travel insurance and can help you choose the right coverage for your trip. We work with reputable insurance providers to offer comprehensive protection.

Can you customize packages for special occasions?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom packages for honeymoons, anniversaries, birthdays, and other special celebrations. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by package and destination. Generally, cancellations made 60+ days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. Please review specific terms when booking.
